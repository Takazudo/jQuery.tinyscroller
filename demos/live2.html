jQuery.tinyscroller

Live with two configs

This live binds let you allow changehash about all in-page anchors without [href="#all"].
I wanted this because "back to top" links are not to anchor something.

$.Tinyscroller({ changehash: false }).live('a[href="#all"]');
$.Tinyscroller({ changehash: true }).live('a[href^="#"]:not([href="#all"])');
